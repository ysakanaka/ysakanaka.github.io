Research

The improvement of artificial immune algorithm Opt-IA through surrogate assistant model

Artificial immunity algorithm Opt-IA is one of the algorithms classified into complex systems inspired by the immune system of vertebrates. In this research, first, we tested the performance of Opt-IA with one of the simplest problems, OneMax Problem and applied to travelling salesman problems. According to those result, we found that there was still room for improvement in the aspect of computational complexity and to escape from local optimum. Currently, we are trying to apply 4 new methods including surrogate assistant model using Gaussian Process to improve Opt-IA.

Publication: Yuko Sakanaka, Nathanael Aubert-Kato, ”Surrogate-Assisted Optimization of the Opt-IA Artificial Immune System Algorithm”. 2019 IEEE Symposium Series on Computational Intelligence.
